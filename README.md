# Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

**PCEA Trinity Church* has a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation.

We prohibit:
- The creation, distribution, or sharing of Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM)
- Sexualization of minors in any form
- Grooming or exploitative behavior involving minors

Violations will result in account termination and reporting to authorities.
